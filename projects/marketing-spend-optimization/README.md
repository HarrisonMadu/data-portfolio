# 📊 Marketing Spend Optimization – Data Analysis

**Objective:** Analyze 6 months of marketing data to identify the most effective channels and recommend budget allocation.

**Key Skills Demonstrated:**
- Data cleaning & exploratory analysis
- Statistical modeling (linear regression)
- Data visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Business storytelling & recommendations

**Results:**
- Identified Email Marketing as the highest ROAS channel (4.0x)
- Recommended budget shift from TikTok → Email/Google
- Potential revenue increase of ₹1.2L/month with no extra spend
- R² = 0.87 for revenue prediction model

**Tools:** Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn), Jupyter Notebook

**📁 Files:**
- `analysis.ipynb` – Full analysis notebook
- `marketing_data.csv` – Sample data (generated)
